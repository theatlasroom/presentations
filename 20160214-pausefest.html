Card sorting

Why?

  • Starting point for a series of community focused activities to help with the re-development of the NJC website
  • Test the existing website content with community
  • Get some ideas on possible services and information we might have not thought of
  • Most importantly, design something that works for the people who need it, you and everyone else in our community
