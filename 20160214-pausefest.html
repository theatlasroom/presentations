Imagine a government website designed for, and by its users

  • The NJC are embarking on a website redesign and would like this new site to be people-focused from the ground up
  • So how would you design a website for people who have to go to court, people who might be seeking help and treatment services, or want to host a community event?

  • Raise your hand if you've done a card sort before?
  • Basically, its as the name suggests, you'll be sorting cards, grouping and labelling them. Simple right?
  • The cards cover a range of current information and services that describe the NJC, as well as some services that havent commenced yet

Lets begin

  • Look at the personas you have been given
  • Look through your cards, discuss, ask questions

5mins

Grouping

Group your cards, talk through this process

  • Use the blank cards to add or replace existing cards
  • Which of these do you feel go togethor?
  • Are any groups more important than others?
  • Any cards you are unsure about - put aside

There are no right or wrong answers

15mins

Nice one, next...

  • Refine your groups
  • Label your groups
  • If you put cards aside, is there a way you can label them?
